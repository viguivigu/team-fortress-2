TF2

A class-based first-person shooter released in 2007 by Valve. In that game, you fight for RED (Reliable Excavation Demolition) against BLU (Builders League United), or else you can fight for RED against BLU. Yeah, that's pretty much it. Anyway, at Halloween, you can fight three bosses, and while you are fighting them, a truce mode will be on until the boss is defeated. Why, you may ask? Because you will complete contracts when you beat the bosses and get a free hat for completing the contracts!!! That's why the player count often rises on Halloween. Welp, we discussed the game itself, but now we will go and look at the game modes!

Game modes

there are 9+ gamemodes, so you will have a choice what to play.

Capture The Flag(CTF)

Grab a briefcase with intelligence at the enemy base and run back to yours before the enemy team demolishes you, surely there won't be any engineers nesting in intel room

Control point (CP)

In this gamemode you'll need to fight off the control points from the opposing team. but there are two types of this gamemode! why won't we compare them? basically in Symmetrical the maps are symmetrical and no team has upper ground in amout of control points captured at the start of the game and in Attack/Defend the maps aren't symmetrical and RED have upper ground with all control points in their hands and they need to survive some time with at least one control point in their possession to win the game and BLU need to capture all of the control points to their side

King Of The Hill (KOTH)

It's like the control point gamemode but you only have 1 control point and you need to capture and defend it before the time runs out

Mann Vs Machine (MVM)

Basically there are a grey dude and he is not a fan of the mannco so he decides to destroy it. you will need to pass trough multiple waves full of robots that are simmilar to the TF2 classes

Payload (PL)

There are 2 types of this gamemode but basically you need to push a big cart with explosives to the end and destroy enemy base with it! Be careful, some demomans can put sticky bombs under the cart so be careful

Special Delivery (SD)

You grab australium and load them on the point

Territorial Control (TC)

It's like the symmetrical Control Point gamemode but not symetrical, you run around, capture the control points and try to survive

Playable classes

there are 9 classes in total, varying from attack to support and defence.

Scout

The first class, attacker. he is fast and has low hp, captures control points and moves payload twice as fast as others. good for taking out classes one by one and fastly capturing things. has scattergun, pistol and bat as stock weapons

Soldier

The second class, attacker. he is not fast but can be mobile via rocket jumps. main attacking power on crowds. has rocket launcher, shotgun and showel as stock weapons

Pyro

The third class, attacker. counters soldiers easily if they don't use direct hit. easily gets enemy medics away from their teams. main attacking power focused on burning the enemies in close range. flamethrower, shotgun and fire axe as stock weapons

Demoman

The fourth class, defender. can make traps with his secondary weapon or just spam projectiles. can become a subclass named demoknight if you equip boots, shield and sword. focus on protecting payloads and control points. has pipebomb launcher, sticky bomb launcher and bottle as stock weapons

Heavy

The fifth class, defender. minigun go ratattatatattatatata, enemy dead. good at defending control points. countered by scout. minigun, shotgun and fists as stock weapons

Engineer

The sixth class, defender. builds turrets, dispencers and teleporters at the cost of metal. good at defending static things (control points and intellegence. spy is not a big problem for the "nesting" engineers. shotgun, pistol, wrench, construction PDA and destruction PDA as stock weapons

Medic

The seventh class, support. can heal teammates and give them UBERCHARGE, granting them invincibility for some seconds. syringe gun, medigun and bonesaw as stock weapons

Sniper

The eighth class, support. stays at high range and tries to hit you in the head usually. countered by moving side to side and pressing ctrl key. sniper rifle, SMG and kukri as stock weapons

Spy

The ninth and the last class, support. counters the other supports and remembers that he has a revolver sometimes. revolver, sapper, knife, invisibility watch and diguise kit as stock weapons.
